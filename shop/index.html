New Book | 2024: A Daily Photography Practice

2024: A Daily Photography Practice is a self-published photobook by Ben W. Fey which documents his daily photography practice. Over the 366 days of 2024, Ben made and shared 412 photographs which are all included in the book.

About Ben W. Fey

Ben W. Fey is a designer and photographer based in Charlottesville, Virginia. He fondly remembers taking photographs of the Indiana Jones Stunt Spectacular at Disney as a kid in the mid-90s. One of his favorite photographs was not of the fire or action, but of the place where Indiana Jones hid when the giant ball rolled down the hill. Almost 30 years later, Ben still loves photographing the overlooked details around him. His work has been exhibited in Charlottesville at New City Arts Welcome Gallery and Second Street Gallery. Ben holds a Bachelor of Science in Architecture from the University of Virginia.
