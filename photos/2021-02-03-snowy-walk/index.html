Photo Essay: Snowy Walk in the Neighborhood

3 February 2021
Charlottesville, VA

We woke up to snow on Sunday morning. After attending virtual church because of COVID-19, we put on our ski gear and went for a walk in the neighborhood. It was satisfying to make the first steps in the fresh blanket of snow as we went for our walk. It was good to run into neighbors during our walk, to hear and see the neighborhood children enjoying the hilly terrain, and to have moments of silence in the cool, crisp air.
