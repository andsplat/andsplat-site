366 Photography Project: March Review

I am working on a 366 photography project this year after a creative drought. Each month I am looking back on the photographs I took during the month and selecting my favorite five photographs. In this post, let's look back at the photographs I made during March 2024.

March also brought the addition of a new lens for my Nikon Zfc. On 2 March I received the Sigma 56mm f/1.4 lens and ended up using that lens for every photo for the rest of the month. I am really enjoying the focal length (84mm, 35mm equivalent), wide aperture, and build quality of this lens.
